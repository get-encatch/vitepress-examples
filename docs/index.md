Encatch enables you to capture user experiences in the flow and at the moment — on web and mobile, without sending people to an external form.

Beyond capturing feedback, Encatch also helps you capture user intent: what someone was trying to do, where they got stuck, and why it mattered, while they are still in your product. Most tools link users away from your app and lose that context. Encatch embeds surveys natively in your UI so the experience stays on your domain, matches your design, and keeps capture tied to the moment.

For a looser integration, Encatch also provides **link-based feedback** — share a form URL from docs, email, chat, or anywhere you already reach users, without a full in-app SDK setup.

## Why teams use Encatch

- **Native experience** — Forms render inside your app, not in a separate window or third-party widget.
- **Configure once** — Create a form in the dashboard, then show it on web, React Native, or Flutter after a one-time SDK install.
- **Link-based feedback** — When you want a lighter integration, use shareable form links instead of (or alongside) embedded capture.
- **Works with your stack** — Route feedback to tools like Jira, Slack, and Salesforce.
